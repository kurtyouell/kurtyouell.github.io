Systems & Tools

Built from scratch.

The infrastructure behind effective preconstruction — tracking tools, estimating systems, proposals, and the processes that make teams actually work.

01 — Dashboards
Dashboards & Analytics

Live pipeline tracking across 52 active projects with $327M in pursuit value. 8-month outlook forecasting, stage analytics, win/loss tracking, and sales summit goal reporting — all driven from a single master sheet.

02 — Estimating
Estimating Systems

End-to-end estimating tools from conceptual through detailed pricing. Member schedules, cost analysis, scope breakdowns, and pricing summaries — built for transparency, repeatability, and rapid iteration under tight timelines.

03 — Proposals
Proposals & Marketing

Client-facing proposals that translate complex mass timber scope into clear, visual, and commercially compelling presentations. Templates, scope matrices, VE analysis, and project timelines — designed to win work.

04 — Models
Sales Models

Dynamic 3D models built in Cadwork to evaluate scope, pricing, and feasibility early in the sales cycle. Enabled faster go/no-go decisions and clearer alignment between sales and operations before engineering is engaged.

05 — Tracking
Project Tracking & Data Standards

Centralized tracking tools for scope, quantities, schedules, and decision status across concurrent projects. Consistent data structures across models enabled cleaner handoffs, automation, and reliable downstream reporting.

06 — Engineering
Concept-Level Engineering Tools

Parametric calculators for early-stage GLT sizing and CLT fire-resistance checks. Supported informed design decisions and go/no-go calls before full engineering engagement — reducing cost and schedule risk at the front end.

07 — Documentation
User Manuals & SOPs

Clear, step-by-step documentation covering Cadwork standards, file naming conventions, SharePoint architecture, and fabrication design workflows. Built to reduce reliance on tribal knowledge and increase team autonomy at scale.

About
